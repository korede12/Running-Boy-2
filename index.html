ⓘ About the Game

Story

A lone skeleton sprints through the rain-soaked city at night — and Godzilla is right behind them. Jump over the beast to survive and rack up points.

How to Play

  • Press SPACE or tap to jump
  • Hold longer to jump higher
  • Clear Godzilla while airborne to score +2 pts
  • Getting hit ends the run

Tips

  • Watch the charge bar — full charge = max height
  • Time your jump just as Godzilla closes in
  • Godzilla sometimes stomps or breathes fire!

Built With

  • Phaser 3 game framework
  • Hand-drawn graphics & particle effects
